For Hipcheck, values matter

We want to help open source maintainers and users understand the dependencies they’re using.

Hipcheck recommends “pass” or “investigate” because people, not automated tools, should decide what to use.

We’re open source ourselves, and care about being transparent, welcoming, and safe for everyone.
